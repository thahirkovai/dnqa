About Us

We take pleasure to introduce ourselves DHARUN NASEEHA QURAN ACADEMY have a dream and vision to make every Tamil speaking Muslim family to know to recite the Quran with TAJWEED and learn Aqeedha & Dheeniyyath, at least who have online facilities with them. For that, we have started online live classes from November 2020 onwards to teach Arabic to read the Quran, via Tamil speaking teachers. We would like to invite all Tamil speaking Muslim families globally to join in our online live sessions through ZOOM/GOOGLE MEET.

Special features:
  • Quran and Sunnah teaching based on Salaf manhaj.
  • Female teachers will conduct classes to girls & homemakers.
  • Quality education in low fees.
